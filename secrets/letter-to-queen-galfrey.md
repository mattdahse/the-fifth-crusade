# A Letter to Queen Galfrey

*The company's dispatch to the sovereign of the Fifth Crusade, sent from Citadel Drezen after the Sword of Valor was raised again in its halls.*

Queen Galfrey,

We are glad to hear you are bringing the fight to the demons in the south, and that our actions have brought hope to the Crusade.

Please know that you have our deepest thanks and gratitude for sending your Steward to us in such timely fashion, and for the accolades we have been given.

Please know that we will do our best to restore the city of Drezen and the Citadel to their former glory, now that the Sword of Valor once again graces the halls of the Citadel.

Horgus has arranged with your Steward to have supplies sent to allow us to maintain our position here in Drezen.

We have suffered very few casualties and defeated several armies of demons and tieflings while taking the Citadel, but we are still severely shorthanded to maintain true control of the area.

Your Steward has let us know that refugees are coming from the south, and that recruitment is being set up to provide additional troops and guard.

Please know that Drezen has not fared well; most of the city and the citadel is little more than wreckage after the treatment the demons have visited upon it these last few years.

If you could put in a word with any of your people in Mendev to send engineers, architects, and builders willing to brave the trip to Drezen — along with any building supplies and coin that could be spared to start the rebuilding effort — it would be greatly appreciated. It is our plan to provide jobs to the refugees in rebuilding Drezen, so that its former splendor might once again shine in this otherwise dreary place.

Paladin Harlock has requested from his church the right and leave to found a new order of the Holy Knights of Iomedae, to be known as Iomedae's Preservers. The mandate of this new order is to defend Drezen and the Sword of Valor, and to increase local recruitment as Drezen expands. If you could see your way to putting in a good word with the Church to grant this license, and to provide any assistance, this too would be greatly appreciated.

We await your command at Citadel Drezen.

Your loyal subjects,

*Rabiah's Redeemers*

Praise Iomedae! Praise Sarenrae!
